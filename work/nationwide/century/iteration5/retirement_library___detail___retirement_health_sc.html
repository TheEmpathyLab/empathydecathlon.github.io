Professional Retirement Health Screening

Planning your finances in retirement isn't just about checking up on your financial health... its important to take stock of your actual health, too.


Working with a medical professional to screen for key health issues can help you take action to reduce your risks for certain diseases and catch issues early—helping you save lots of money in retirement.


Retirement health screenings check for the following:

• Understand your medical and family history to identify key areas to keep an eye on

• Physical examination to examine your vitals, mobility, and vision

• Spirometry to test lung function

• Audiogram to check for hearing defects

• Blood and urine tests to screen for early signs of disease


The outcomes of this screening can be used as an input to personalize your medical expenses and identify opportunities to save on future healthcare costs by reducing your risk of a serious health event.
