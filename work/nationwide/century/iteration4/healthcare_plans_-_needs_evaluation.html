Picking healthcare coverage is sort of like a box of chocolates... you never know what you're going to get...

What's Most Important to Me In a Health Plan:


Keep my branded drugs

I take expenses prescription drugs, and I like them! Help me pick a plan that covers those best.


Get covered no matter what

I like to play it safe--because you never know what might happen. I want coverage that is going to protect me the best (even if it costs me a little more).


Pay the least amount

I don't want to overpay. I want the most cost effective option for me--even though I'll have to pay a lot more if something out of the ordinary happens.


Keep my doctors

I like doctor Sampson--and nurse Jackie! I want coverage that helps me keep my providers, even if it costs me a little extra.
